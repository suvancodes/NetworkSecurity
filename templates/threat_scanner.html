Scan any URL

Paste a website URL and the scanner will fetch the page, extract features and provide a detailed risk analysis with explanations.

What the scanner checks

  • DNS, SSL and hosting information
  • Links, anchors and external resources
  • Form handlers, popups and iframe usage
  • Traffic & page rank signals
  • Per-feature explanation for human review

Quick tips

  • Include protocol (https://) for best results
  • If a scan fails, try Manual mode
  • Trusted domains are auto-marked safe
  • Large sites may take a few seconds to analyze

Examples

Try:

google.com example.com phishy-example.test

Notes for analysts

Results include both automated signals and heuristic detections. Always review the feature breakdown before taking action on high-impact decisions.
